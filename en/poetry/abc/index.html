abc

1/6/2021

A and B and also C
Three letters of agony
You have caused me
O, you gave me
The worst possible epiphany

“Pronouncing these letters three
Would bring forth of knowledge sea—”
Quoted he and I
(Not realizing his lie)
Exclaimed them like I would a plea

Now I stand in front of thee
Cruel ghost of the library
Let me go—
Let me leave
What I once picked up in glee
