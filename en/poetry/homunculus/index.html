Homunculus

8/5/2020

His skin of paper, ink and stone
Is stronger than the toughest bone
Of worries to him knives are least
Nil but fire can stop this beast
Nil but fire — and the light of days
Are strong enough to set ablaze
A body born of rose’s thorns
Grown and raised in catacombs
